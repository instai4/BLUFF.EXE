How To Play
Each player gets 6–8 cards. Rest become the Draw Pile.
On your turn, play 1+ cards face-down and announce their rank. You may bluff!
Round Lock: Once a rank is played, everyone MUST claim that same rank until Bluff is called.
ANY player can shout "Bluff!" — not just the next player.
Bluff caught = liar picks up pile. Wrong call = accuser picks up pile.
Pass costs 1 Draw Pile card. Cannot pass before first play.
If you draw a card matching the locked rank — you get a bonus turn!
Playing all 4 of a rank = FULL SET — next player gets free pass immunity!
First to empty their hand wins!
