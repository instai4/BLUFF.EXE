  How To Play — Rules
Each player receives 6–8 cards. Remaining cards form the Draw Pile.
On your turn, place 1 or more cards face-down and announce their rank. You may bluff!
ANY player can call "Bluff!" after a play — not just the next player.
Bluff caught: the liar picks up the entire pile. Wrong call: the accuser picks it up.
Passing your turn costs 1 Draw Pile card as penalty.
Playing all 4 cards of the same rank grants the next player a FREE pass — no draw penalty!
If the Draw Pile empties, discard pile reshuffles into a new Draw Pile.
First player to empty their hand wins the game!
